Coronavirus Symptoms

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization. Most common symptoms:

  • fever
  • dry cough
  • tiredness
Less common symptoms:
  • aches and pains
  • sore throat
  • diarrhoea
  • conjunctivitis
  • headache
  • loss of taste or smell

From World Health Oorganization News Report

9 May 2020
Russia reports record daily increase in coronavirus cases since the beginning of the pandemic

Russia reported 12,126 new cases of Covid-19 on Friday, the highest daily increase recorded since the beginning of the pandemic, according to the data from the country's coronavirus response headquarters.

"Over the past 24 hours there have been 12,126 confirmed cases of new coronavirus infection Covid-19 in 85 regions, including 3,272 (27%) active cases without clinical manifestations,” the statement from health officials said.
In total, Russia has reported 1,272,238 coronavirus cases as of October 9. Russia’s deputy prime minister Tatyana Golikova has previously said that May 11 was the peak of the pandemic, with 11,656 officially reported cases on that day.
